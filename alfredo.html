Tagliatelle Alfredo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g tagliatelle
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook tagliatelle in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in grated parmesan cheese, allowing it to melt into a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper, then toss in cooked tagliatelle.
  6. Mix until well coated, then serve with fresh parsley if desired.
