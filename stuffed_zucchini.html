Delicious Stuffed Zucchini Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 250g ground beef or mixed meat
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 50g grated cheese (Mozzarella or Gouda)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cut zucchinis in half lengthwise and carefully scoop out the flesh.
  3. Chop the zucchini flesh and mix it with ground meat, onion, garlic, tomato, and tomato paste.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  5. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the mixture briefly.
  6. Fill the zucchini halves with the mixture and top with grated cheese.
  7. Bake for about 25 minutes until the cheese is golden brown.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!
