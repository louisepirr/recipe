Shrimp Scampi Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g linguine or spaghetti
  • 250g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter with olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and shrimp, cooking until pink and fully cooked.
  4. Pour in white wine and let simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Toss in cooked pasta and coat well with the sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
