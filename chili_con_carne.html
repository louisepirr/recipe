Hearty Chili Con Carne Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (400g) red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and bell pepper until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, diced tomatoes, kidney beans, and spices.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve hot with rice, tortilla chips, or bread.
