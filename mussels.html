Mussels Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti or linguine
  • 500g fresh mussels, cleaned
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 200ml dry white wine
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cherry tomatoes, cooking until softened.
  4. Pour in white wine and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add mussels, cover, and cook until they open (discard any that remain closed).
  6. Toss in cooked pasta, lemon juice, and fresh parsley, mixing well.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra parsley and black pepper.
