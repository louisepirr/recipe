Authentic Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground lamb or beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground meat, garlic, onion, parsley, and spices.
  2. Shape the mixture into long, thin patties around skewers.
  3. Brush with olive oil.
  4. Grill on medium-high heat for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Serve with flatbread, salad, or a yogurt-based sauce.
