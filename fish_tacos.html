Delicious Fish Tacos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 white fish fillets (cod, tilapia, or mahi-mahi)
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • ½ cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill or pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Mix chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Rub seasoning onto fish fillets and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Grill fish for 3-4 minutes per side until flaky.
  5. Warm tortillas and assemble with fish, cabbage, tomatoes, cilantro, and avocado.
  6. Mix sour cream with lime juice and drizzle over tacos.
  7. Serve fresh with lime wedges.
