Classic Homemade Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard

Instructions:

  1. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Worcestershire sauce.
  2. Shape into 4 patties and let rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill or pan over medium heat and cook patties for 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Add cheese slices in the last minute of cooking (if using).
  5. Toast burger buns lightly.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauces.
  7. Serve hot with fries or coleslaw.
