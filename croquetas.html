Traditional Croquetas Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup finely chopped ham or chicken
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and stir in flour.
  2. Slowly whisk in milk, stirring until thick and smooth.
  3. Add chopped ham or chicken, salt, pepper, and nutmeg, mixing well.
  4. Let the mixture cool, then shape into small croquettes.
  5. Dip each croqueta in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry until golden brown.
