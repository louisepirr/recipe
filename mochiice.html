Classic Mochi Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sweet glutinous rice flour (mochiko)
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Cornstarch or potato starch (for dusting)
  • 1 ½ cups ice cream (any flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Scoop small balls of ice cream onto a tray lined with parchment paper. Freeze for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix rice flour, sugar, and water until smooth. Cover and microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Stir the mixture, then microwave for another minute. Stir again, then microwave for 30 more seconds until sticky and translucent.
  4. Dust a surface with cornstarch and roll out the mochi dough into a thin layer. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  5. Cut circles from the dough using a cookie cutter. Place an ice cream ball in the center and wrap the mochi around it, pinching the edges to seal.
  6. Wrap each mochi ice cream ball in plastic wrap and freeze for at least 2 hours.
  7. Let them sit at room temperature for a few minutes before eating. Enjoy!
