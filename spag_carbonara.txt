        <h1>Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe</h1>
        <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>400g spaghetti</li>
          <li>200g pancetta, diced</li>
          <li>2 large eggs</li>
          <li>50g pecorino cheese, grated</li>
          <li>50g parmesan cheese, grated</li>
          <li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
          <li>Salt & black pepper to taste</li>
          <li>Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)</li>
        </ul>
        <h3>Instructions:</h3>
        <ol>
          <li>Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.</li>
          <li>While pasta is cooking, saut√© pancetta in a pan until crispy.</li>
          <li>In a bowl, whisk eggs with pecorino and parmesan cheese, adding black pepper.</li>
          <li>Drain pasta and toss it in the pan with pancetta and garlic.</li>
          <li>Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg-cheese mixture, stirring until creamy.</li>
          <li>Serve immediately, garnished with parsley if desired.</li>
        </ol>
