Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 120g unsalted butter, softened
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 50g white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 150g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in vanilla and egg until combined.
  5. Gradually mix in dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool on a wire rack before enjoying.
