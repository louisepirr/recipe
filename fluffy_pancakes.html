Japanese Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs (separated)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 30g (¼ cup) all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Butter (for cooking)
  • Powdered sugar & maple syrup (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Separate egg yolks and whites into two bowls.
  2. Whisk yolks with milk, vanilla, flour, and baking powder until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, beat egg whites with sugar and lemon juice until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the egg whites into the yolk mixture, maintaining the airiness.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan on low, melt butter, and scoop batter into thick pancakes.
  6. Cover and cook for 4-5 minutes per side until golden and fluffy.
  7. Serve dusted with powdered sugar and drizzled with maple syrup.
