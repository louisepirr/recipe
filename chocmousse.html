Classic Chocolate Mousse Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 8 ounces semisweet baking chocolate, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Beat egg yolks in a small bowl with an electric mixer on high speed for about 3 minutes until thick and lemon-colored.
  2. Gradually beat in sugar.
  3. Heat 1 cup of whipping cream in a saucepan over medium heat until hot.
  4. Slowly stir at least half of the hot cream into the egg yolk mixture, then return everything to the saucepan.
  5. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens (do not boil).
  6. Stir in chopped chocolate until melted, then refrigerate for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. Beat the remaining 1 ½ cups of whipping cream until stiff peaks form.
  8. Fold whipped cream into the chocolate mixture.
  9. Pipe or spoon into serving bowls and refrigerate
