Mushroom Risotto Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 300g Arborio rice
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 50ml white wine
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  3. Stir in Arborio rice and toast for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour in white wine and let it absorb.
  5. Gradually add vegetable broth, stirring continuously until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Mix in parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.
