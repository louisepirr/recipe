Spinach & Ricotta Cannelloni Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 cannelloni tubes
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 200g fresh spinach, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • 100g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, spinach, garlic, parmesan, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Stir in beaten egg to bind the filling.
  4. Fill each cannelloni tube with the ricotta mixture.
  5. Spread half the tomato sauce in a baking dish and arrange filled cannelloni on top.
  6. Pour remaining tomato sauce over the cannelloni and top with shredded mozzarella.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.
