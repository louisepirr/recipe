French Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of thick bread (brioche or challah works best)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Maple syrup & powdered sugar (for serving)
  • Fresh berries (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook bread slices for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh berries.
