        <h1>Authentic Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe</h1>
        <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>3 skinless chicken breasts, cubed</li>
          <li>½ cup plain yogurt</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons lemon juice</li>
          <li>4 cloves garlic, minced</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons ginger, minced</li>
          <li>2 teaspoons salt</li>
          <li>2 teaspoons ground cumin</li>
          <li>2 teaspoons garam masala</li>
          <li>2 teaspoons paprika</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons oil</li>
          <li>1 large onion, chopped</li>
          <li>1 teaspoon turmeric</li>
          <li>1 teaspoon ground coriander</li>
          <li>1 teaspoon chili powder</li>
          <li>1 tablespoon tomato paste</li>
          <li>1 can (400g) diced tomatoes</li>
          <li>1 cup heavy cream</li>
          <li>¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped</li>
        </ul>
        <h3>Instructions:</h3>
        <ol>
          <li>Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, salt, cumin, garam masala, and paprika. Marinate for at least 1 hour.</li>
          <li>Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) and bake marinated chicken for 15 minutes until slightly charred.</li>
          <li>Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions until soft.</li>
          <li>Add turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and tomato paste, stirring for 30 seconds.</li>
          <li>Pour in diced tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.</li>
          <li>Stir in heavy cream and add cooked chicken.</li>
          <li>Simmer for another 5 minutes, garnish with cilantro, and serve with rice or naan.</li>
        </ol>
