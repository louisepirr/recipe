Gnocchi with Pesto Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g potato gnocchi
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 50g pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Â½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional, halved)

Instructions:

  1. Cook gnocchi in salted boiling water until they float, then drain.
  2. In a food processor, blend basil, pine nuts, parmesan, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Season the pesto with salt and black pepper.
  4. Toss cooked gnocchi with the pesto sauce until well coated.
  5. Garnish with halved cherry tomatoes if desired and serve immediately.
