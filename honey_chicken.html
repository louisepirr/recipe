Delicious Honey Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • ½ cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 1 tablespoon water (if using cornstarch)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix honey, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, ginger, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken in a baking dish and pour sauce over it.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, basting occasionally.
  5. If thickening sauce, mix cornstarch and water in a small bowl, then stir into sauce.
  6. Serve hot with rice or vegetables.
