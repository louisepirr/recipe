Greek Yogurt Parfait Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup granola
  • ½ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt and granola.
  2. Add a layer of mixed berries.
  3. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup.
  4. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Top with chopped nuts for crunch.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.
