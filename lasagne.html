Lasagna Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 150g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 150g mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
