Classic Tuna Niçoise Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans tuna (in olive oil, drained)
  • 4 small potatoes, boiled and sliced
  • 2 cups green beans, blanched
  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, arugula, etc.)
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, quartered
  • ½ cup black olives
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Arrange mixed greens on a large plate.
  3. Top with potatoes, green beans, tomatoes, eggs, tuna, and olives.
  4. Drizzle with dressing and toss gently.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy!
