Traditional Kassler Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 smoked pork loin (Kassler)
  • 1 pound potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 14 ounces sauerkraut
  • 1 cup veal or beef stock
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Pat Kassler dry and place in a baking dish.
  3. Add sauerkraut and stock, then cover and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, boil potatoes in salted water for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Drain potatoes and mash with warm milk, butter, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Slice Kassler and serve with mashed potatoes and sauerkraut.
