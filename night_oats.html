Overnight Oats Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • ¼ cup Greek yogurt (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup fresh fruit (bananas, berries, or apples)
  • ¼ cup nuts or granola (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. In a jar or bowl, mix oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, sweetener, and vanilla.
  2. Stir well and seal the container.
  3. Refrigerate overnight or at least 6 hours.
  4. Before serving, add fresh fruit and crunchy toppings.
  5. Enjoy cold or slightly warmed up!
