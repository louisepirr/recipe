Classic Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 4 green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee in a bowl.
  2. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Shape the dough into small balls, ensuring they are smooth without cracks.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the balls until golden brown, stirring gently for even cooking.
  6. In a separate pot, boil sugar, water, and cardamom until slightly sticky.
  7. Turn off the heat and add rose water to the syrup.
  8. Soak the fried gulab jamun in warm syrup for at least 2 hours.
  9. Garnish with chopped pistachios and serve warm.
