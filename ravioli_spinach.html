Ricotta & Spinach Ravioli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g fresh pasta sheets
  • 200g ricotta cheese
  • 150g fresh spinach, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg, beaten (for sealing)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Butter & sage sauce (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ricotta, spinach, garlic, parmesan, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Cut fresh pasta sheets into squares, placing small spoonfuls of filling in the center.
  3. Brush edges with beaten egg and seal ravioli by pressing firmly.
  4. Boil ravioli in salted water until they float, about 3 minutes.
  5. In a pan, melt butter and add sage leaves, simmering until fragrant.
  6. Drizzle butter sauce over cooked ravioli and serve warm.
