Croissant Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold, for layering)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add warm milk and knead into a smooth dough; refrigerate for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out dough and layer it with cold butter, folding and chilling between folds.
  4. Repeat folding and chilling to create flaky layers.
  5. Roll out and cut into triangles, then roll into croissant shapes.
  6. Place on a baking sheet, brush with egg wash, and let rise for 2 hours.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 18-20 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Enjoy warm, preferably with a cup of coffee!
