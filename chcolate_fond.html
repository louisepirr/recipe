Chocolate Fondant Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 80g sugar
  • 50g flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
  3. Whisk the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Mix in the melted chocolate and butter, then add the flour.
  5. Pour the mixture into greased ramekins.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, ensuring the center remains gooey.
  7. Enjoy your delicious chocolate fondant!
