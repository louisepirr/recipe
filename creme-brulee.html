Classic Crème Brûlée Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar (plus extra for topping)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Heat cream until almost boiling, then slowly whisk into the egg mixture.
  4. Strain the mixture and pour into ramekins.
  5. Bake in a water bath for about 30-40 minutes, until set but slightly jiggly.
  6. Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  7. Sprinkle brown sugar on top and caramelize with a torch.
  8. Enjoy your creamy, delicious crème brûlée!
