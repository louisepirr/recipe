Avocado Toast with Poached Egg Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain or sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (for poaching)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (chives or parsley) for garnish
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mash avocado in a bowl, season with salt, black pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  3. In a pot, bring water to a gentle simmer and add vinegar.
  4. Crack an egg into a small bowl, then carefully slide it into the water.
  5. Poach for 3-4 minutes until whites are set and yolk is runny.
  6. Spread mashed avocado on toast and top with poached egg.
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with cherry tomatoes if desired.
