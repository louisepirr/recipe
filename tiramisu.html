Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 200ml strong coffee
  • 200g ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the coffee and let it cool.
  2. Separate egg yolks and whites.
  3. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until creamy, then mix in mascarpone.
  4. Whisk egg whites separately until stiff, then fold them into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer them in a dish.
  6. Spread a layer of mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then repeat.
