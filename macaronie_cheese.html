Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 200g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Breadcrumbs for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat, then stir in flour to create a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, cooking until slightly thickened.
  4. Stir in cheddar, parmesan, mustard, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  5. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix thoroughly.
  6. Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs if using.
  7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
