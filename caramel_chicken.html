Caramel Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Spring onions & sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and brown the chicken on both sides.
  2. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add sugar and cook until melted and caramelized.
  4. Stir in garlic, soy sauce, fish sauce, ginger, pepper, and water.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and simmer until fully cooked and coated with caramel glaze.
  6. Add rice vinegar, stir well, and cook for another minute.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds before serving.
