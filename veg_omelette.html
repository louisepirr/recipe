Vegetable Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ cup diced bell peppers
  • ¼ cup diced onions
  • ¼ cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ cup fresh spinach
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Saute onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  5. Pour eggs over the vegetables and let cook until set.
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top if using, then fold the omelette in half.
  7. Cook for another minute, then serve warm.
