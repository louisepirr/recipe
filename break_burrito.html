Breakfast Burrito Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 large tortilla
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese
  • ½ cup cooked breakfast sausage or bacon
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • ¼ cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a pan until cooked.
  2. Warm the tortilla in a dry pan.
  3. Layer eggs, cheese, sausage, avocado, and tomatoes on the tortilla.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and optional hot sauce.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla, then roll it up tightly.
  6. Toast the burrito in the pan for a crispy finish.
  7. Enjoy your breakfast burrito warm!
