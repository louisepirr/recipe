Traditional Hungarian Goulash Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 large potatoes, diced
  • 2 large carrots, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, paprika, and caraway seeds, stirring for 1 minute.
  3. Brown beef cubes in the pot, then stir in tomato paste.
  4. Pour in beef broth and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer uncovered for about 1.5 to 2 hours until beef is tender.
  7. Serve hot with crusty bread or dumplings.
