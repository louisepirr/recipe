Classic Fish and Chips Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 white fish fillets (cod, haddock, or pollock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup cold sparkling water
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar (optional, for serving)
  • Salt (for seasoning)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Gradually whisk in sparkling water until smooth.
  4. Dip fish fillets into batter, coating evenly.
  5. Fry fish for 4-5 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Fry potato fries until golden and crispy, then season with salt.
  8. Serve hot with vinegar or tartar sauce.
