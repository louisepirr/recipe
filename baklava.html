Classic Baklava Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound phyllo dough
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts or pistachios
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup honey
  • ¾ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish.
  2. Layer phyllo sheets in the dish, brushing each with melted butter.
  3. After 5 layers, sprinkle a portion of the nut mixture (nuts + cinnamon).
  4. Repeat layering phyllo and nuts until all ingredients are used.
  5. Cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes until golden brown.
  7. While baking, prepare the syrup by heating sugar, honey, water, and lemon juice until dissolved.
  8. Pour the syrup over the hot baklava and let it soak.
  9. Allow to cool completely before serving.
