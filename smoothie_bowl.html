Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana, sliced and frozen
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt or plant-based alternative
  • ½ cup almond milk (or preferred milk)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Granola, sliced fruit, and nuts for topping

Instructions:

  1. Blend frozen banana, berries, yogurt, milk, and sweetener until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and let it sit for a minute to thicken.
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds, granola, sliced fruit, and nuts on top.
  4. Enjoy immediately with a spoon!
