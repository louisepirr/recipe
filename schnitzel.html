Classic Schnitzel Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork or veal cutlets
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • ½ cup vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 lemon, sliced (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Pound the cutlets to about ¼-inch thickness.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Dredge each cutlet in flour, then dip into beaten eggs, and coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry schnitzels for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with lemon slices.
