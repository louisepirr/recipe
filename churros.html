Classic Churros Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart vegetable oil (for frying)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water, sugar, salt, and vegetable oil to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in flour until the mixture forms a dough.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pan to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Transfer dough to a piping bag fitted with a star tip.
  5. Pipe strips of dough into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels and roll in cinnamon sugar.
  7. Serve warm with chocolate sauce or caramel for dipping.
