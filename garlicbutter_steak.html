Garlic Butter Steak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks (or your preferred cut)
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon paprika

Instructions:

  1. Let steaks sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Season steaks with salt, pepper, thyme, rosemary, and paprika.
  4. Place steaks in skillet and sear for about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Reduce heat, add butter and garlic, and spoon over steaks while cooking.
  6. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
