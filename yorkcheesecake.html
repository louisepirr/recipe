Classic New York Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 24 oz (680g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into the pan to form the crust.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Blend in sour cream, vanilla extract, and flour until smooth.
  6. Pour the mixture over the crust and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, until the center is just set.
  8. Turn off the oven, crack the door slightly, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
  10. Enjoy your rich and creamy New York Cheesecake!
